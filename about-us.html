Features of Hello Superstars














   Hello Superstars is an innovative social media application, which builds the bridge between superstars and their fans.

   Hello Superstars is an amusement tool, which has been designed for the working class people, and expatriates with a very low fee of in-app purchase, as low as 0.50 cent or $1 (or INR50 or INR100) or even lower this price.

   Hello Superstars is a mobile application where superstars will register themselves to upload their unique activities, which can be followed by the fans who are also registered with the Hello Super Stars app.

   Hello Superstars facilitates both registered stars and followers to share their moments and memories through live chat video

   Hello Superstars creates the virtual platform for the talents in domains of acting, music, sports etc so that they can upload their videos, which will be judged by the panel of top super stars.

   Hello Superstars initiates the online learning/training platform where the fans will learn from their favourite celebrities.

   Hello Superstars creates an e-commerce platform where the celebrities will be able to display their different items to be purchased online by fans.

   Hello superStars organizes e-talent hunt/e-audition for the deserving fans who possess certain level of talent/skills.
