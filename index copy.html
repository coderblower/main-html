Trade and Remittance Expo

In addition, the intention is to promote Bangladesh trade and enhance the remittance flow from Qatar to Bangladesh. in this expo we will promote Bangladeshi products and services to the potential buyers not only from Qatar but also from entire gulf countries. We will strongly promote the remittance issue to stump out the touts who are encouraging Bangladeshi Expatriate to remit through illegal channel.

Read More

Grand Concert

Beside the trade and remittance expo there is also a cultural festival to provide a complimentary entertainment programme for all expatriates with a apecial focus on Bangladeshi community who are living in Qatar with the determination of promoting Bangladesh remittance and local products.

Read More

Business Summit

This is considered as B2B, which gathers the business owners, leaders or experts from various industries to discuss and exchange ideas on business-related topics from both countries of Bangladesh and Qatar. The goal of this business summit programme is to share knowledge, explore new trends, and foster collaboration among participants to trigger export and import between two countries.
